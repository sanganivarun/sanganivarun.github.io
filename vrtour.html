Virtual Reality Tour

Summary

This is a Android VR Application I created using Unity and Android Studio.
C# is used for scripting. It is an informative app for students wishing to pursue their education in BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus. It was a part of my thesis project. The scenes contain 360 degree images that can be viewed through head rotation in Cardboard VR. It uses gaze input to move around the campus.
